Generative AI creates new content such as text, images, code, and audio.
 
Large Language Models (LLMs) are trained on massive amounts of text and generate human-like responses.
 
Embeddings are numerical vector representations of text that capture semantic meaning.
 
SentenceTransformer converts text into embeddings.
 
ChromaDB stores embeddings and retrieves semantically similar documents.
 
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) first retrieves relevant information from a vector database and then sends that information to an LLM.
 
Prompt engineering improves the quality of responses by providing instructions, context, and constraints.
 
REST APIs allow applications to communicate with LLM services using HTTP requests.
